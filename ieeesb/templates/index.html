  • IEEE

    IEEE, being the world's largest technical/professional society, continuosly promotes the development and application of electrotechnology and allied sciences for the advancement of the profession. With the 4,30,000 members spanning across 165 countries of the globe, IEEE fosters the development of standards, that often become national and international standards and produces over 30% of the world's literature in its related fields, publishing over 100 peer-reviewed journal.

    IEEE Nagpur Subsection

    Coming into existence, The Nagpur Subsection was officially established on 3rd September 2017. It consists of technical professionals as well as students from various institutions. The agenda behind the formation of this sub-section is to torch the paths of Engineering students and proffesionals, and prepare them for the competent world out thereand to accept the globally posed challenges confidentally, via varios Events that we organize to update knowledge and enhance skill sets.

    JDCOEM

    J D College of Engineering and Management has been established with the primary objective of imparting technical and management education of high standards aiming at creating professionals par excellence. JDCOEM is affiliated to RTM Nagpur University and can boast of University rank holders in its very first graduating batch, 2011-12. With Excellence as its mission and Merit as its Motto, JDCOEM is fast evolving as one of the best institutions in the region. From 2017 JDCOEM affiliated with Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technical University, Lonere. And in 2019 the JDCOEM awarded as Autonomous Status by UGC. JDCOEM offers Graduate and post-graduate programs in many core branches in the disciplines of Engineering and Management.
